Delete Your Account

We’re sorry to see you go. This page explains how to permanently delete your VitaPaw account and all associated data.

How to request deletion

Email us from the email address associated with your VitaPaw account and include the phrase “Delete My VitaPaw Account” in the subject line.

Send to: Yummin.shin@gmail.com

Tip: If you’re emailing from a different address, please state the account email clearly so we can verify ownership.

What will be deleted?

  • Your account details (name, email, city, date of birth, profile image).
  • Authentication credentials (password is hashed; credentials removed).
  • All pet profiles and pet health data (breed, DOB, weight, allergies, health goals/conditions, poop status, neutered status, activity/body condition, favorites, medical records).
  • AI meal generations, edits, and selections tied to your account.
  • Subscription records and delivery history (addresses, statuses).
  • Notification tokens associated with your devices.
  • Any other data linked to your user ID stored by VitaPaw.

Data retention

After your request is confirmed, your data will be deleted from our servers within 7 business days. Any backups containing your data will also be purged during this period.

Need help?

If you have questions about your data or this process, contact us at Yummin.shin@gmail.com. You can also review our Privacy Policy for more details on how we handle your information.
