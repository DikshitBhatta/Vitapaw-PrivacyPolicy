Privacy Policy – VitaPaw

Last Updated: August 23, 2025

Welcome to VitaPaw. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our pet meal personalization app (“App”). We ensure compliance with Apple’s App Store Guidelines, GDPR, and CCPA.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Information: Name, email, password, city, date of birth, and profile image (for account setup and personalization).
  • Pet Data: Pet name, breed, weight, age, gender, activity level, health conditions, allergies, poop status, neutered status, and health goals (used to generate AI-based meals).
  • Subscription & Delivery Data: Meal selections, subscription details, addresses, and delivery updates.
  • Device & App Data: Device model, OS version, crash logs, and in-app activity (for analytics and bug fixes).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To generate AI-personalized pet meals using your provided pet data.
  • To process subscriptions and manage delivery of meals.
  • To send important notifications (e.g., subscription status, delivery updates).
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance features.

3. AI Processing

VitaPaw uses Gemini AI to generate personalized meal recommendations. AI processes only the data required (pet profile + available ingredients) and does not permanently store sensitive pet or user data.

4. Data Linked to You

The following types of data may be linked to your identity (as per App Store privacy labels):

  • Contact Info (name, email, city)
  • User Content (pet profile, subscription preferences)
  • Identifiers (Firebase UID)
  • Diagnostics (crash logs, performance data)

5. Data Not Collected

We do not collect your financial data, browsing history, or unrelated health data. We do not track users across other apps or websites.

6. Permissions

  • Location: Used only to help with delivery addresses and logistics (optional, managed in iOS settings).
  • Notifications: Used to keep users informed about subscriptions, meal readiness, and delivery progress.

7. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with trusted providers like Firebase (authentication, database), cloud hosting, and logistics partners for delivery purposes.

8. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Personal and pet data stored securely in Firebase/Firestore.
  • Account access is secured via Firebase Authentication.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services. You can request account and data deletion anytime:

Delete Account: Delete Your Account

Your data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

10. Children’s Privacy

VitaPaw is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn a child has provided data, it will be deleted promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this policy. If significant changes are made, users will be notified in-app or by email.
