VitaPaw ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered pet meal personalization app. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the VitaPaw mobile application (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data generated by your use of the App to deliver meal personalization, manage subscriptions, and coordinate delivery.

A. User Account Information

  • At Sign-Up: name, email address, password, city, date of birth, and profile image.
  • Profile Updates: you may edit your user information within the App.

B. Pet Data (for AI meal personalization)

  • Pet name, breed, date of birth, weight, gender.
  • Activity level, body condition, health goals.
  • Allergies, favorite foods, health conditions, medical records, poop status/condition, neutered status.
  • Other characteristics you add to improve personalization.

C. Subscription, Inventory, and Delivery Data

  • Subscription Details: chosen meals, edits/regenerations, plan type, frequency, status.
  • Delivery Information: addresses and contact details for dispatch, midway status, and delivered status.
  • Admin & Operations: admin role assignment, ingredient/inventory updates, order stages (preparing / ready), approvals and operational notes.

D. Device & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, app version, and crash/diagnostic logs.
  • In-app actions related to meal generation, subscription management, and delivery tracking.

2. How We Use Information

  • AI Personalization: generate, edit, and improve pet meals using your pet data and current inventory data.
  • Subscription Management: process new subscriptions, admin approvals, modifications, pauses, and cancellations.
  • Delivery Operations: coordinate dispatch, midpoint updates, and confirmation of delivery.
  • App Improvements: monitor performance, fix bugs, and enhance features.
  • Communications: send updates about subscriptions, deliveries, or policy changes.

3. AI Processing (Gemini)

The App uses an AI model (Gemini). The AI processes a combination of pet data you provide and ingredient inventory configured by admins to propose meal formulations. We apply data minimization by sending only the fields needed for the task. AI outputs are used within VitaPaw to help you select, edit, or regenerate meals.

Note: Where applicable, AI processing may be performed by our cloud providers under their privacy and security terms, acting as processors on our behalf.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only as described below:

  • Service Providers: authentication, database, hosting, AI processing, analytics/crash reporting, and delivery/logistics partners (e.g., Firebase/Firestore, cloud & AI infrastructure, delivery partners). These providers process data under contractual safeguards.
  • Legal & Safety: where required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of VitaPaw, our users, or others.

5. Security

  • Data in transit is protected via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication handled via secure methods (e.g., Firebase Authentication).
  • Personal and pet data stored in managed cloud databases with industry-standard protections.
  • Access controls applied to admin functionality and operational data.

6. Permissions & Consent

The App may request the following permissions. You can grant or revoke them in your device settings.

  • Location: used to support delivery (e.g., determining delivery to your current or saved address). The App does not continuously track your location in the background unless you explicitly enable such a feature.
  • Notifications: used to send subscription status updates (e.g., admin approval), meal preparation stages (preparing/ready), and delivery updates (dispatched/midway/delivered) to users; and operational alerts to admins.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with the services. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by following the steps below:

Delete Account: To delete your account and all associated data, please visit: Delete Your Account. All personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

  • Access & Update: view and update your profile, pet data, and subscription settings in the App.
  • Deletion: delete your account and associated data via the link above.
  • Portability: request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format by contacting us.

10. International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where applicable, we implement safeguards to protect your information consistent with applicable laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will notify you within the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
